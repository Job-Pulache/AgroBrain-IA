#AgroBrain IA

##General description

AgroBrain IA is an intelligent platform designed for agribusinesses that centralizes operational information, analyzes productivity indicators,, and generates recommendations for decision-making.

The platform aims to reduce reliance on manual reports, improve operational visibility, and support supervisors and managers through data-driven analysis and recommendations.

##Problem

Agribusinesses generate large amounts of information related to production, agricultural tasks, personnel, quality, and perfomance.

However:
-The information is scarttered across multiple systems.
-There are delays in generating reports
-User depend on analysts to obtain specific information
-Problems are often detected only after they have alreadyy occurred.
-Decision-making relies primarily on staff experience.

##General objetive
To develop an intelligent platform capable of consulting, analyzing, and recommending operational actions using real-time agricultural and production information

##Specific Objectives
-Centralize operational indicators
-Automate frequently asked questions
-Detect productivity deviations
-Generate early warnings
-Recommend corrective actions
-Improve decision-making
-Reduce reliance on manual reports
