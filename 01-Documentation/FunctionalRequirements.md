# Functional Requirements - AgroBrain AI

## RF-001 User Management

The system shall allow for the registration, modification, querying, and deactivation of users.

---

## RF-002 Role Management

The system shall allow for the assignment of access roles to users.

Roles:

* Administrator
* Manager
* Field Manager
* Supervisor
* Analyst

---

## RF-003 Farm Management

The system shall allow for the registration and management of agricultural farms.

---

## RF-004 Plot Management

The system shall allow for the registration of plots associated with a farm.

---

## RF-005 Worker Management

The system shall allow for the registration of workers and their association with crews.

---

## RF-006 Crew Management

The system shall allow for the registration of crews and the assignment of supervisors.

---

## RF-007 Production Logging

The system shall record:

* Date
* Farm
* Plot
* Crew
* Worker
* Variety
* Export crates
* IQF crates
* Culls/Discard crates

---

## RF-008 Operational Dashboard

The system shall display operational indicators in real-time.

---

## RF-009 Productivity Alerts

The system shall automatically detect drops in productivity.

---

## RF-010 Recommendation Engine

The system shall generate recommendations to improve operational productivity.

---

## RF-011 Intelligent Queries

The system shall allow for natural language queries.

Example:

"Which farm has the least progress?"

"Who is the most productive worker?"
